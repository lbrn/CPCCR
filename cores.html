MBIPC Core

The Molecular Biology and Immunopathology Core (MBIPC) and all 4 Center projects are located within the LSU SVM. MBIPC will be operationally headquartered within the existing administrative structure and laboratories of the Division of Biotechnology and Molecular Medicine (BioMMED).

PCEC Core

The Pre-Clinical Evaluation Core (PCEC) primarily serves as a centralized resource center for assessment of cancer disease mechanisms and cancer models that are studied by the individual PJI projects.
